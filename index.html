01 — About

Cutting frames is technical.
Telling a story is everything.

I'm Jeeva A, a video editor based in Tamil Nadu working with brands, agencies, and creators to turn raw footage into content people actually stop scrolling for. My work spans short-form reels, long-form YouTube videos, and wellness & brand campaigns — always with an eye on pacing, rhythm, and the small details that make an edit feel premium.

My journey started with hands-on videography — managing framing, lighting, and sound on set — before moving fully into the edit suite. That on-set experience shapes how I cut: I think about coverage, intention, and story before I think about transitions.

At Genie Magnet and Mindei, I've edited everything from fast social reels to full campaign series, working in CapCut, DaVinci Resolve, and Canva, with a growing focus on motion graphics and color grading using Adobe's creative suite.

  • Storytelling-first editing approach, built on real on-set experience
  • Fast turnaround without compromising visual quality
  • Platform-optimized exports for Instagram, YouTube & client delivery
  • Comfortable collaborating directly with marketing & creative teams
